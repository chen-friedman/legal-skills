## 13. Особенности — Notes

- Russian uses Cyrillic script. Don't mix with Latin in keyword matching.
- Date formats common: `DD.MM.YYYY` (e.g., `15.03.2025`)
- Numeric formats may use space or non-breaking space as thousands separator
