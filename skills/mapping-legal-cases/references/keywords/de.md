**Status:** Loaded when corpus primary language is `de` (German).

## 13. Notes

- German dates: `DD.MM.YYYY` is standard (e.g., `15.03.2025`)
- Month names: Januar, Februar, März, April, Mai, Juni, Juli, August, September, Oktober, November, Dezember
- Decimal separator: `,` (comma)
- Thousands separator: `.` or space
- Compound nouns common in legal writing (e.g., `Schadensersatzanspruch`)
