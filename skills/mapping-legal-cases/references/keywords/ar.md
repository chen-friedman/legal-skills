**Status:** Loaded when corpus primary language is `ar` (Arabic).

Extends `common-en.md`. General-purpose Arabic legal terminology covering civil, criminal, family, commercial, and labor law. Terminology varies across jurisdictions — this pack focuses on widely-shared Modern Standard Arabic terms. Jurisdiction-specific packs (Egypt, Saudi Arabia, Jordan, UAE, Palestine) can be added.

## 13. رمز الاتجاه — Directionality Note

Arabic is RTL (right-to-left). When writing output in Arabic:
- Keep bullet-point / table structure intact
- File paths stay LTR (don't mirror)
- Dates: prefer ISO format or Arabic numerals in Western order
