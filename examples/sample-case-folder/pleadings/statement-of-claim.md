# STATEMENT OF CLAIM

**Case No.:** 2025-CV-0042
**Court:** Superior Court, Central District (fictional)

**Plaintiff:** John Doe, residing at 123 Sample Street, Example City
**Defendant:** Acme Corporation Ltd., with offices at 456 Demo Avenue, Example City

**Filed:** 2025-03-15

---

## I. Parties

1. Plaintiff John Doe is the owner and lessor of the commercial premises located at 789 Case Road, Example City ("the Premises").

2. Defendant Acme Corporation Ltd. is a company duly registered, acting through its CEO Ms. Jane Smith.

## II. Jurisdiction

3. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to the Lease Agreement (Section 14.1) which designates the Central District Superior Court as the exclusive forum.

## III. Facts

4. On 2023-01-15, Plaintiff and Defendant executed a three-year commercial lease (the "Lease") for the Premises at a monthly rent of $5,000.

5. Defendant was obligated to pay rent on the first day of each month.

6. From 2024-09-01 onwards, Defendant failed to make timely payments. As of the filing date, outstanding rent totals $45,000 covering 9 months.

7. On 2024-11-20, Plaintiff's attorney sent a formal demand letter, to which Defendant replied on 2024-12-05 refusing payment and citing alleged maintenance deficiencies.

8. Plaintiff has at all times fulfilled obligations under the Lease.

## IV. Claims

### Count 1 — Breach of Contract

9. Defendant's failure to pay rent constitutes a material breach of the Lease.

10. Plaintiff seeks damages of $250,000, consisting of:
    - Outstanding rent: $45,000
    - Accelerated rent for remaining lease term: $180,000
    - Interest and costs: $25,000

### Count 2 — Declaratory Relief

11. Plaintiff seeks a declaration that the Lease is terminated effective 2025-04-01.

## V. Relief Sought

Plaintiff respectfully requests:

a. Judgment in the amount of $250,000;
b. Declaration that the Lease is terminated;
c. Attorneys' fees and costs;
d. Such other relief as the Court deems just.
