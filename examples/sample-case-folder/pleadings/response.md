## Affirmative Defenses

**First Defense — Set-off:** Defendant is entitled to offset repair costs of $28,500 against rent per Lease § 8.2.

**Second Defense — Constructive Eviction:** Plaintiff's failure to maintain the Premises rendered them partially unusable, excusing performance.

**Third Defense — Prior Breach:** Plaintiff's material breach of maintenance obligations preceded any payment default, relieving Defendant of performance per the doctrine of prior material breach.

## Counterclaim

Defendant counterclaims for $28,500 in unreimbursed maintenance costs plus consequential damages of $15,000 arising from business disruption.
